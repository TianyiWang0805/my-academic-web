Research Experience

  • Embedded Hardware Development for GroGenesis V2 Greenhouse Sensor System
    Research Assistant (Mitacs Globalink Research Internship), July 2025 – Present

  • S-TCN Neural Networks for Gas Detection: Algorithm Design and Implementation on Edge Devices
    Key Contributor, June 2024 – July 2025
    Built a multi-channel gas sensor array and designed a 4-layer PCB.
    Developed an S-TCN model using dilated depthwise separable convolutions to improve accuracy and efficiency.

  • Rational Design and Fabrication of MEMS Gas Sensors toward Long-Term Stability: A Comprehensive Review
    Key Contributor, Sept. 2024 – Apr. 2025
    Reviewed MEMS gas sensors and identified core failure modes affecting long-term operational stability.
    Analyzed material degradation mechanisms and evaluated stable electrode/sensing materials through literature mining and theoretical comparison.
  • Humidity-Tolerant Chemiresistive Hydrogel Sensor for Real-Time Breath CO2 Monitoring
    First Author & Leader, July 2024 – Mar. 2025
    Synthesized and optimized dimethylamine-functionalized hydrogels with enhanced CO2 sensitivity, mechanical stretchability, and humidity-assisted ionic conductivity.
    Designed a flexible PCB with integrated microcontroller, analog interface, and wireless modules for real-time CO2 signal acquisition and transmission.
    Integrated the sensor into a wearable smart mask and developed a mobile interface for synchronized respiratory, activity, and metabolic monitoring.
  • Design and Development of the Ion-Sensitive Field-Effect Transistor (ISFET) PH Sensor
    Leader, Sept. 2023 – Dec. 2024
    Designed an epitaxial-gate ISFET sensor with a separated chip-packaging architecture to enhance long-term stability, replaceability, and signal isolation.
    Developed a compact signal acquisition circuit integrating low-noise amplification, temperature compensation, and reference electrode interfacing for stable pH readout.
